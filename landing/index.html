Questions

Good to know.

How does Gradaroo work?

Pick your Australian university and Gradaroo shows the organisations its graduates commonly work for, then pulls live job openings at those employers. You can filter by area of interest, or upload your resume for an AI skills match.

Is Gradaroo free?

Yes — completely free, with live graduate job listings across Australia. If it helps you, there's an optional Ko-fi tip jar, but nothing is paywalled.

Which universities are covered?

40 Australian universities, sorted by QS World University Ranking, from Charles Darwin University and the University of Melbourne through to regional institutions.

What happens to my resume?

Smart match is optional. If you upload a resume, its text is read by a third-party AI service to identify your skills. We don't store your resume.
